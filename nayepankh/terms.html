Terms & Conditions

By accessing or using the NayePankh Foundation website, applying for an internship, or making a donation, you agree to the following terms.

  • NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government, 80G & 12A registered non-governmental organisation operating on a volunteer and donor-supported basis.
  • Internship roles, durations, and stipend structures are subject to change based on programme needs and may vary by role.
  • Certificates and letters of recommendation are issued at the discretion of the foundation, based on satisfactory completion of assigned responsibilities.
  • All content on this website is for informational purposes; the foundation reserves the right to update programme details at any time.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

  • Information collected through application or contact forms (name, email, phone number) is used solely to process your application or respond to your enquiry.
  • We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.
  • Donor information is kept confidential and used only for donation processing, receipts, and tax documentation.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by writing to contact@nayepankh.com.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

As a non-profit organisation, our cancellation and refund approach is guided by the nature of charitable contributions.

  • Donations made to NayePankh Foundation are generally non-refundable, as funds are often allocated to ongoing programmes shortly after receipt.
  • In case of a genuine payment error (duplicate transaction, incorrect amount), please contact us within 7 days at contact@nayepankh.com with your transaction details.
  • Internship applications may be withdrawn at any time before confirmation without penalty by informing your assigned mentor or our team.
