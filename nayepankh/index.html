About Us

Think global. Act local.

NayePankh Foundation began as a small group of students in Uttar Pradesh who believed that compassion shouldn't wait for permission. Today, we're a UP Government, 80G & 12A registered NGO and one of India's largest student-led organisations — built entirely by young people who give their time, skills, and energy to lift others up.

We work directly with underprivileged families across Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and several other cities — distributing food, sanitary hygiene kits, clothing, and educational support to those who need it most.

What We Do

Four pillars, one purpose.

01
Food Distribution

Regular meal and ration drives for families and street communities who go without, run entirely by volunteer hands.

02
Menstrual & Hygiene Kits

Free sanitary pads and hygiene essentials for girls and women who'd otherwise have to choose between dignity and daily needs.

03
Clothing Drives

Collecting and distributing clothes through donation camps that turn closets into warmth for someone else's winter.

04
Education Support

Books, stationery, and mentorship for children whose families can't otherwise afford the basics of schooling.
