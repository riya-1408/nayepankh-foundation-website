Good To Know

Frequently asked questions.

Is this internship paid?

It depends on the role. Fundraising internships work on a performance-based stipend model — typically a percentage of funds you help raise. Other roles like web development, content, and design are unpaid but come with a certificate, letter of recommendation, and genuine portfolio-worthy experience.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Most roles are open to students from any year of study, including first-years and recent graduates. We care more about consistency and willingness to learn than a polished resume.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes. Every intern who completes their internship receives a digital certificate. Strong performers are also offered a letter of recommendation from the foundation.

Can I do this alongside college or a job?

Most internships are remote with flexible hours, designed specifically so students can contribute around their existing classes or commitments.

How long does the application process take?

Typically a few days from application to a short screening call. Once confirmed, you'll be onboarded with a mentor who guides you through your first week.

Don't see a perfect fit?

We're always open to motivated students. Reach out and tell us where you'd like to contribute.
