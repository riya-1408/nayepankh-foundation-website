Our Story

Built by students. Driven by belief.

NayePankh means "new wings." It's the idea that with the right support at the right moment, anyone can rise — and that the people best placed to offer that support are often the ones still figuring out their own path too.

"NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all." — Founding Charter

Who We Are

A movement that started small.

NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government, 80G & 12A registered NGO, and one of the biggest student-led organisations in India. What began as a handful of students sharing meals with people on the street has grown into a structured foundation working across Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and several other cities.

We've never had a large paid staff or a corporate office. Our strength has always been volunteers and interns — young people who show up, again and again, because the work matters more than the title.

Every rupee donated and every hour volunteered goes toward one simple goal: giving someone who's been overlooked a fair shot at dignity.

Our Journey

How NayePankh took flight.

2018

A handful of students, a shared meal drive

What started as informal food distribution by a small group of college students in Uttar Pradesh became the seed of NayePankh.

2020

Official registration as an NGO

NayePankh Foundation was formally registered with the UP Government, and began operating with structured donation and volunteer drives.

2021–22

80G & 12A certification, expansion to Ghaziabad

Donations became tax-exempt under 80G, and operations expanded beyond Kanpur into Ghaziabad and neighbouring cities.

2023

Media recognition

Our work was featured in The Pioneer, Dainik Jagran, Hindustan, and other regional press — bringing wider attention to the cause.

2024–26

30,000+ interns trained, 2 lakh+ lives touched

The internship program scaled nationally, becoming one of the largest student volunteering pipelines for social work in India.

What Drives Us

The values behind the work.

Compassion First

Every decision starts with the question: does this make someone's life a little easier today?

Radical Transparency

Every donation is tracked, every drive documented. Trust is earned through receipts, not promises.

Student-Led, Always

Young people run this organisation — making decisions, leading drives, owning outcomes from day one.

Action Over Talk

We measure ourselves in meals served and kits delivered, not in mission statements.

Consistency

Real change comes from showing up every month, not just once during a festival season.

Open Doors

Anyone willing to give time or skill has a place here — no resume required, just intent.

"We don't ask for much — just help us with what you can. Be it money, skill, or your time."

Prashant Shukla
Founder & President, NayePankh Foundation
